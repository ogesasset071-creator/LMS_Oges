PLATFORM OVERVIEW
This platform is an LMS for internal training and skill development.
It helps trainers manage courses, assignments, quizzes, submissions, announcements, and learner progress.
It helps learners discover trainings, watch lessons, save notes, use resources, complete assignments, track progress, and earn certificates.
